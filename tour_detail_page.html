üìñ Tour Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the breathtaking Swiss Alps, where majestic mountains meet crystal-clear lakes and charming alpine villages. This carefully curated 7-day adventure takes you through some of Switzerland's most iconic destinations, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural experiences, and outdoor activities.

From the cosmopolitan city of Zurich to the adventure capital of Interlaken, from the car-free village of Zermatt to the stunning Jungfraujoch - the Top of Europe, every day brings new wonders and unforgettable memories. Experience traditional Swiss hospitality, savor authentic cuisine, and immerse yourself in the alpine lifestyle.

Whether you're hiking through flower-filled meadows, cruising on pristine mountain lakes, or simply taking in the panoramic views, this tour showcases the very best of the Swiss Alps in all their glory.

üóìÔ∏è Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich

Welcome to Switzerland! Upon arrival at Zurich Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, enjoy a welcome orientation session and meet your fellow travelers. In the evening, take a leisurely walk along Lake Zurich and explore the charming Old Town with its medieval architecture, boutique shops, and cozy cafes. Welcome dinner at a traditional Swiss restaurant.

2

Day 2: Zurich to Lucerne

After breakfast, journey to the picturesque city of Lucerne. Visit the iconic Chapel Bridge, the Lion Monument, and explore the charming old town. Take a relaxing cruise on Lake Lucerne, surrounded by stunning mountain scenery. Optional: Mt. Pilatus cable car excursion for panoramic views. Evening free to explore Lucerne's vibrant cultural scene and waterfront promenade.

3

Day 3: Lucerne to Interlaken

Travel through the scenic Bernese Oberland to Interlaken, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. Afternoon at leisure to explore this adventure capital - try paragliding, visit the Harder Kulm viewpoint, or simply stroll through town. Optional activities include boat cruises, mountain biking, or visiting nearby waterfalls. Evening briefing for tomorrow's Jungfraujoch excursion.

4

Day 4: Jungfraujoch - Top of Europe

Today's highlight: journey to Jungfraujoch, Europe's highest railway station at 3,454 meters. Experience the incredible cogwheel train ride through the heart of the mountain. At the top, visit the Ice Palace, Sphinx Observatory, and enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of the Aletsch Glacier and surrounding peaks. Weather permitting, enjoy snow activities year-round. Return to Interlaken in the evening.

5

Day 5: Interlaken to Zermatt

Depart for the car-free alpine village of Zermatt, home to the iconic Matterhorn. Travel by scenic train through the Rhone Valley. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the afternoon exploring this charming village with its traditional wooden chalets and electric taxis. Enjoy mountain views from various vantage points and visit the Matterhorn Museum. Traditional fondue dinner experience.

6

Day 6: Zermatt & Gornergrat

Take the cogwheel train to Gornergrat (3,089m) for spectacular views of the Matterhorn and surrounding 4,000-meter peaks. Multiple viewing platforms offer photo opportunities of 29 mountain peaks. Optional activities include short hikes, visiting the glacier palace, or simply relaxing at mountain restaurants. Afternoon free for last-minute shopping or spa time. Farewell dinner with group.

7

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast and hotel check-out, transfer to Zurich Airport for your departure flight. Take with you unforgettable memories of the Swiss Alps and friendships made along the way. Optional: Extend your stay to explore more of Switzerland independently.
